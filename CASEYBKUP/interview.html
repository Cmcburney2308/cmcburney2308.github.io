Creator Details

Basically judt the process of making this website, and my logs through it

Representation & Audience

This page is all about my cousin Casey, who I’m showing off as a kind of celebrity for my Media Studies project. I’m Cameron McBurney, and I wanted the site to feel a bit like those celebrity magazine interviews you see online, but still easy for people our age to get into. I kept the same layout, fonts and colours from the homepage so it all ties together. The text is short, friendly and straight to the point. I’ve made sure everything here is original and with Casey’s say-so. I didn’t want to overload it with boring personal info, so instead I’ve put the focus on Casey’s work, the stuff that inspires her, and how she connects with people. Basicly, it’s all about making her feel like a star.

Everything on this website was completely on the consent of Casey, she had a choice in everything as i thought it was only right. thanks for viewing my work!

Behind the Scenes

The 'Behind The Scenes' was originally just supposed to be some random photos of that weird stage in town, but when i realised that all photos have to be original i changed my directive to a more vague and general approach.
